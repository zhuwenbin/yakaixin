# App Store Review Response - Guideline 5.1.1

Dear App Review Team,

Thank you for your feedback. We appreciate your review and would like to clarify that our app **already supports unauthenticated users to freely browse content without registration or login**.

## Features Available Without Login

Unauthenticated users can access the following features:

1. **Browse Home Page** - View all product listings with prices, descriptions, and images
2. **View Product Details** - Access complete product information, course outlines, and pricing
3. **Browse Question Bank** - Explore question bank products and daily practice items
4. **Browse Courses** - View online courses and live streaming content
5. **Select Major** - Choose different majors to browse relevant content

## Features Requiring Login

Login is only required for account-based features:
- Purchasing products (to manage orders)
- Starting practice/learning (to save progress)
- Check-in function (to record data)
- Personal center (to view learning statistics and order history)

## User Experience

When unauthenticated users attempt account-based actions (such as purchasing), they see a friendly prompt: "This operation requires login. Please log in first" with options to "Go to Login" or "Cancel". Users can choose to continue browsing without logging in.

## Testing Steps

To verify unauthenticated access:
1. Launch the app (first install or clear data)
2. You will enter the home page directly - no login required
3. Browse products, view details, and check prices - all accessible
4. Try tapping "Purchase" - a login prompt appears (non-forced)
5. Tap "Cancel" - you can continue browsing

## Compliance

Our app fully complies with Guideline 5.1.1:
- ✅ Users can browse content without providing personal information
- ✅ Login is only required for account-related operations
- ✅ Login prompts are clear and non-forced
